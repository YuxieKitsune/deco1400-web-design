The BFG

by Roald Dahl

Gobblefunk: The Dahl Dictionary

  • The BFG

    The BFG - or, to give him his full name, The Big Friendly Giant, is the smallest of all the Giants in Giant Country - but he is still 3 to 4 times larger than the tallest human on the planet! He is also the kindest Giant in the Giant Country and catches nice dreams to share with sleeping children.

  • The Witching Hour

    The snozzcumber resembles a cucumber. It has the most repulsive flavour that resembles frogskin and rotten fish.
